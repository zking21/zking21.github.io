Nutella Hot Chocolate

Total time: 5 minutes


Ingredients List

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tbsp. Nutella
  • 1 tbsp. cocoa
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in a small saucepan.
  2. Stir over medium heat until all ingredients are combined and Nutella is melted.
  3. Serve in your favourite mug.
